How much does it cost to hire a maid ?
A detailed cost comparison of visa options in U.A.E and relevant costs to hire a maid

It is possible to obtain a maid visa through authorized Tadbeer centers or Tas-heel. The visa is available in two options, Tadbeer visa or direct family visa. The table below provides an explanation of the difference in cost. While Tadbeer visa is much cheaper than direct family visa, some families prefer to opt for direct visa as it gives full control. Note that each Tadbeer center has a slightly different Visa package, google tadbeer visa then contact Tadbeer centers, ask for the cost of visa.
